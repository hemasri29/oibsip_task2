Mother Teresa

   

"Peace begins with a smile"

Mother Teresa was born on August 26, 1910 in Skopje, a city situated at the crossroad of the Balkans.

Mother Teresa is known for her missionary work in helping the poor. She was an empathetic devout catholic nun whose mission was to lessen the mystery that sick and poor went through.

Some facts about her:

  • She became a nun at the age of 18.
  • She lived and worked in India for 17 years.
  • She had incredible organizational skills along with her compassion and love for humanity.
  • She was a Nobel Peace Prize winner.
  • She was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 1979.
  • She is considered to be the greatest humanitarian to have ever lived in the 20th century.
  • On September 4, 2016, Mother Teresa was canonized as a saint a day before the 19th anniversary of her death.
  • Died on september 5,1997 in kolkata

"It's not how much we give but how much love we put into giving"-Mother Teresa
